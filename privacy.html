CFD Penney

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2025

TL;DR: CFD Penney is designed with privacy in mind. We don't collect personal data, don't track you, and don't sell your information. Your annotations and collaboration sessions are peer-to-peer and not stored on our servers.

IMPORTANT - YOUR RESPONSIBILITY

USER RESPONSIBILITY FOR DATA PROTECTION:

While we respect your privacy and minimize data collection, YOU ARE RESPONSIBLE for protecting your own sensitive information:

  • PEER-TO-PEER TRANSMISSION - Your content is transmitted directly to other participants. Once shared, we cannot control what they do with it
  • NO ENCRYPTION GUARANTEE - While WebRTC uses encryption, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Do not share highly sensitive information
  • SCREEN SHARING RISKS - When sharing your screen, other participants can see everything visible on your screen. Close sensitive windows first
  • NO CONTENT STORAGE - We don't store your drawings. If you need to keep your work, export it locally before ending your session
  • PUBLIC SESSIONS - If you share your session code publicly, anyone with the code can join. Share codes only with trusted participants
  • NO RECOVERY - Once a session ends, content cannot be recovered. Save important work locally

USE COMMON SENSE AND PROTECT YOUR SENSITIVE INFORMATION. WE ARE NOT LIABLE FOR DATA YOU CHOOSE TO SHARE.

1. Introduction

Welcome to CFD Penney ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our collaborative whiteboard and screen annotation tool.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

CFD Penney is designed to work without requiring user accounts or personal information. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Account credentials or passwords
  • Payment information (the service is free)
  • Personal identifiers

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use CFD Penney, we may automatically collect limited technical information:

  • Connection Data: Temporary session codes used to facilitate peer-to-peer connections
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous, aggregated data about feature usage to improve the service
  • Technical Information: Browser type, device type, and operating system (for compatibility purposes)

2.3 Collaboration Session Data

CFD Penney uses WebRTC for peer-to-peer communication:

  • Your drawings and annotations are transmitted directly between participants
  • Session codes are temporary and expire after your session ends
  • We do not store your drawings, annotations, or screen content on our servers
  • Screen sharing and video calls are peer-to-peer and not recorded

3. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Facilitate peer-to-peer connections between users
  • Improve the functionality and performance of CFD Penney
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Ensure service security and prevent abuse

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Peer-to-Peer Architecture

CFD Penney uses a peer-to-peer (P2P) architecture powered by WebRTC:

  • Your data is transmitted directly between participants, not through our servers
  • We only facilitate the initial connection between peers
  • Once connected, all data flows directly between participants

4.2 Local Storage

CFD Penney may use browser local storage to:

  • Remember your preferences (theme settings, tool selections)
  • Cache session codes temporarily
  • Store drawings locally if you choose to work offline

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it in a collaboration session.

4.3 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures:

  • WebRTC connections are encrypted by default
  • Session codes are randomly generated and temporary
  • We use HTTPS to secure connections to our servers
  • Regular security updates and patches

5. Third-Party Services

CFD Penney relies on the following third-party services:

  • PeerJS: For peer-to-peer connection facilitation (open-source library)
  • Font Awesome & Lucide: For icon libraries (no tracking)
  • Google Fonts: For web fonts (may collect basic analytics)

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

6. Cookies and Tracking

CFD Penney does not use cookies for tracking purposes. We may use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality (session management)
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings (theme, tool preferences)

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party tracking cookies.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. We may disclose information only in the following circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid legal processes
  • Safety and Security: To protect the rights, property, or safety of CFD Penney, our users, or others
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, sale, or transfer of assets

8. Children's Privacy

CFD Penney does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about data we may have
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your local storage data (via browser settings)
  • Opt-Out: Disable local storage or cookies through your browser settings
  • Portability: Export your drawings and annotations locally

10. International Users

CFD Penney is available worldwide. If you access our service from outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in countries where we operate. By using CFD Penney, you consent to such transfers.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting a notice on our website
  • Continuing to use CFD Penney after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

12. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • GitHub: github.com/codefrydev/CFDPenney
  • Issues: Report privacy concerns on GitHub

Summary: CFD Penney is built with privacy as a core principle. We minimize data collection, use peer-to-peer connections to keep your data private, and never sell your information. Your collaboration sessions are your own.
